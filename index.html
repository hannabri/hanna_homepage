Six-month internship between January and July 2025 to build a recommandation system for the CPQ tool in ENOVIA's PLM portfolio.The steps followed during the internship were the following:

  • Creation of a simple user interface allowing an easier way to ask questions.
After completing the RAG system, I worked with an internal Business Intelligence system for data analysis.
